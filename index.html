Areas of Practice

🏛️Civil Suits

I represent clients in a wide range of civil disputes including property disputes, contract enforcement, recovery suits, defamation, and injunction matters. With deep procedural knowledge and strategic litigation skills, I ensure efficient resolution while protecting your legal interests.

⚖️Criminal Matters

From bail applications to trial defense, I handle criminal litigation for clients accused of offenses under the IPC and other criminal statutes. I advocate for fair treatment and robust defense in cases involving theft, assault, cybercrimes, and more.

👪Matrimonial Disputes

I offer legal counsel and representation in complex family disputes involving domestic violence, separation, dowry issues, and maintenance. I aim for amicable solutions wherever possible while asserting your rights effectively.

🔗Divorce

Whether mutual or contested, I provide compassionate yet firm legal guidance for divorce proceedings. I assist in alimony negotiations, property division, and documentation, ensuring a smooth legal process during emotionally challenging times.

👶Child Custody

Custody battles require sensitivity and strength. I help parents assert their custodial rights, secure visitation orders, and resolve guardianship matters in the best interest of the child under the Guardians and Wards Act or personal laws.

🛍️Consumer Cases

I assist consumers who’ve faced unfair trade practices, service deficiencies, and fraud. I represent clients before District, State, and National Consumer Forums, helping them receive refunds, replacements, or compensation quickly and legally.

💸 Section 138 NI Act

I specialize in cheque bounce cases under the Negotiable Instruments Act. I ensure timely filing of complaints, effective presentation of evidence, and legal strategy to either recover dues or defend against prosecution.
